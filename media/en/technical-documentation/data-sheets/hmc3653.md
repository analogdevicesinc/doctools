single positive supply: +5V

low noise figure: 4.0 dB  [1]

12 lead 3x3 mm smT package: 9mm²

## General Description

The  HmC3653lp3Be    is  a  HBT  Gain  Block  mmiC amplifier  covering  7  GHz  to  15  GHz  and  packaged in a 3x3 mm plastic Qfn smT package. This versatile amplifier can be used as a cascadable if or rf gain stage in 50 ohm  applications. The HmC3653lp3Be delivers 15 dB gain, and +15 dBm output p1dB with only 4 dB noise figure.
